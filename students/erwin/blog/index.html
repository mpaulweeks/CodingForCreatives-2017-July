What a BLOG Wants!




Whenever I’m on the road, I try to pick up a book to keep me occupied. You might think that I’m all glam and a “diva”, but I do love reading about how to make a site. (Yes, I made this!) Maybe in between studio albums, I can make some extra dough as a website designer! If you’re a newbie like me, check out “Don’t make me think” by Steve Krug.

The first five chapters are fairly straight-forward. Krug stresses out that you don’t want to frustrate your visitors by making them THINK. This reminds me when I had to simplify my songs and dumb it down (GENIE IN A BOTTLE). Keeping it simple is key, because it reduces the noise level of the page and allowing the users to see more of the page.

Thanks again for stopping by and remember, keep it clean, obvious, and universal for web design....but stay dirrty and stretch dem notes on stage LOL!!
